Rising Junior studying at New York University Stern School of Business with concentrations in Computing & Data Science and Management & Organizations.


About Me
======
Data generation occurs everywhere regardless of the industry. As a result, I became intrigued with the various potential applications that data can have, especially in the business industry, which base many decisions on the data of the corporation and others that affect its services. I’m passionate about identifying the challenges people and businesses face, breaking them down analytically, and using data-driven insights to develop effective solutions. To this end, I developed my skills to navigate various applications and coding languages (Python, R, Excel, SQL, Tableau, Power BI) while also attending NYU activities like case competitions to further my problem-solving, interpersonal team communication, and presenting skills.

On campus, I enjoy giving back to the community, participating as a Welcome Leader to assist incoming students and am excited to work as a Media Commuter Assistant next year to plan events and design graphics for my fellow commuter students!


In my free time, I enjoy drawing, reading (recommend me mystery, action, or thrillers!), playing NY Time games, and attending NYC festivals and art markets. Over the summer, you may find me hiking up a mountain and hitting the gym.
